Multimedia and Digital Strategist

Kasia Broussalian is a writer, photographer, videographer and multimedia strategist in New York City specializing in politics, nonprofit work, and environmental issues. Prior to coming to the city, Kasia worked as a documentary photographer and multimedia journalist in Colorado and throughout the Southwest for the Boulder Daily Camera, the Denver Post, the Rocky Mountain News, and the Associated Press. She graduated with an MA in international relations and politics from New York University in 2012, and holds a BS in broadcast journalism from the University of Colorado.

Kasia has been recognized by the Hearst Journalism Awards program, and was chosen as a student for the 2008 Eddie Adams Barnstorm photography workshop. For assignments and inquires, please email Kasia at kbroussalian@gmail.com.
